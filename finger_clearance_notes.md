# Loose finger-clearance version

This version increases the explicit finger-joint clearance so it is less likely to feel press-fit/tight.

## Clearance used
- Material thickness: 3.175 mm
- Assumed kerf: 0.15 mm
- Drawer sliding clearance: 0.25 mm per side, unchanged
- Finger clearance: 0.30 mm total per mating feature
  - Male tabs narrowed by 0.30 mm total
  - Female notches widened by 0.30 mm total
  - Equivalent to 0.15 mm per side

## What this means
A nominal 22.00 mm tab/gap pair becomes:
- male tab: 21.70 mm
- female notch: 22.30 mm

So the theoretical width difference is 0.60 mm between tab and notch, before considering laser kerf. This should be visibly looser than the previous 0.15 mm total clearance version.

## Drawer fit
The overall drawer dimensions were not changed, so the drawer still has:
- 0.25 mm clearance per side inside the outer shell
- 0.50 mm total clearance per axis
